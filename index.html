Equip these rings to reduce explosive damage by 13-25% each. Stack them up to create an even stronger defense against devastating explosions!

RPG Changes: Skill upgrades expanded to Lv. 20 (MAX), matching the previous Lv. 10 (MAX) in strength. You need to reach 200 kills to max all skills now. Vampire effect and Reload Speed effect strength decreased. In Survival mode, the strength of RPG skills remain untouched (except for Vampire).
